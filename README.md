# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: January 5, 2026**

This privacy policy applies to the vocabulary learning app (hereby referred to as "the App") operated by Firewords / Ludwig Crabber.

## Information We Collect

When you use our App, we collect the following information:

### Account Information
- Email address (used for account creation and authentication)
- Authentication credentials (when you sign in with Google or create an account with email/password)

### Usage Data
- Word lists you create
- Practice statistics and learning progress
- Subscription status (free or paying member)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Manage your user account
- Track your learning progress and statistics
- Process subscription payments (if applicable)
- Improve our services

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our App may be used by children under 13. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

### Information We Collect From Children
We collect only the minimal information necessary to provide our service:
- Email address for account creation
- Learning progress and word lists created by the user

### Parental Consent
We require verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information from children under 13. Parents can:
- Review their child's personal information
- Request deletion of their child's information
- Refuse further collection of their child's information

### Parents' Rights
Parents have the right to review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information by contacting us at [Your email address].

We do not:
- Require children to provide more information than necessary to use the App
- Share children's information with third parties for marketing purposes
- Display behavioral advertising to children

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our database. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including children's data.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Sign-In**: For authentication purposes (subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Google Play Billing**: For subscription management (subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))

These services are configured to comply with children's privacy requirements.

## User-Generated Content

Users create their own word lists within the App. We do not pre-screen user-generated content, but we encourage parents to monitor their children's use of the App.

## Data Retention

We retain your account information and learning data for as long as your account remains active. You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us at [Your email address].

## Your Rights (and Parents' Rights for Children Under 13)

You (or parents/guardians for children under 13) have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

## International Users (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Economic Area, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Children under 16 (or lower age as specified by local law) require parental consent.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date. For material changes affecting children's privacy, we will obtain new parental consent where required.

## Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please email us at ludwig.crabber@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include:
- The email address associated with your account
- Confirmation that you want to delete all data

We will process your request within 30 days and delete:
- Your account information
- All word lists you created
- Practice statistics and learning progress
- Subscription information

Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.

- It is also possible to delete your account in the app's account settings pages.
